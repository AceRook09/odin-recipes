Scrambled Eggs

Description

To begin with, these scrambled eggs are pretty easy to make and doesn't take much time to prepare. Furthermore, these scrambled eggs were tweaked according to this recipe, so it should be tasty. Also, the amount of eggs should be just right to get the correct size of the meal. Likewise, having the right amount of spices on these scrambled eggs should satisfy the tastebuds. To end with, these scrambled eggs can be enjoyed by the whole family.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tablespoons sliced Cheddar cheese
  • 2 thin slices ham (Optional)
  • 1 teaspoon heavy whipping cream
  • Â½ teaspoon butter

Steps

  1. Whisk eggs together in a small bowl until smooth. Mix in Cheddar cheese, ham, and heavy cream.
  2. Melt butter in a skillet over medium heat. Pour in egg mixture; cook and stir until set but still moist, 3 to 5 minutes.
